Who We Are

Nairobi's Home for Natural Hair

At Karim Salon & Spa, we celebrate every curl, coil and kink. Nestled in Hurlingham Plaza, we are Nairobi's go-to braiding studio and natural hair care destination — open every single day, walk-ins always welcome.

From butterfly locs to gel nails, our skilled stylists bring precision, creativity and genuine warmth to every appointment. This is your space. Your crown is safe here.
